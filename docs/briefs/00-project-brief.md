# Project Brief: ENEXT Compliance Orchestrator

## 1. Context & Market (Ecuador 2026)
The target market is **Digital Identity Management**, a critical infrastructure in Ecuador.
* **Target Segments:** Banking, Fintech, Insurance, and high-level corporate sectors.
* **Market Size (SAM):** Estimated at **$87.5M USD annually**, driven by electronic signature mandates.
* **Current Catalyst:** Full enforcement of the **Organic Law on Personal Data Protection (LOPDP)** and the rise of sophisticated attacks like *Deepfakes*.

## 2. Core Problem
C-Level executives face the **"Security vs. Experience Paradox"**:
* **Financial Risk:** Fines up to **1% of annual turnover** for LOPDP non-compliance.
* **Operational Friction:** **40% abandonment rates** in digital onboarding due to slow systems or hardware dependence (USB tokens).
* **Security Threats:** Fear of identity theft through synthetic identities or real-time injected videos.

## 3. The Solution: LOPDP Compliance Autopilot
A **"Full-stack" Platform** integrating biometric validation, cloud electronic signature, and an automatic evidence engine.

### Key Value Propositions:
* **Evidence Orchestrator:** Automatic generation of Treatment Activity Records (RAT) and immutable traceability.
* **Invisible Security:** Use of **Passive Liveness Detection** and AI to detect fraud without user friction.
* **Compliance as a Competitive Advantage:** A real-time Dashboard to demonstrate proactive responsibility.

## 4. Business Impact
* **Response:** Reduce onboarding time from days/hours to **minutes**.
* **Integrity:** Elimination of paper/hardware; automatic legal shielding.
* **Conversion:** Reduction of abandonment rates from 40% to 5%.
